Strategic Electrical Consultancy for Commercial & Industrial Projects

Electrical projects within commercial and industrial environments carry significant financial and operational risk. Incorrect specifications, poorly managed contractors, and non-compliant installations can result in project delays, cost overruns, and ongoing safety liabilities. Oleco’s electrical consulting service exists to eliminate these risks.

We provide independent, expert technical advisory to facilities managers, architects, property developers, and project managers who need specialist electrical input — whether or not Oleco performs the physical installation. Our consulting team brings the same rigour and commercial awareness to advisory work as we apply to our own contracting projects.

Who Benefits from Electrical Consulting?

Our electrical consultancy services are designed for organisations and professionals who require specialist technical guidance at any stage of a project. Typical clients include:

  • Facilities managers overseeing multi-site electrical programmes
  • Architects and interior designers requiring electrical specification input
  • Property developers planning new commercial builds or refurbishments
  • Corporate estates teams managing planned maintenance and upgrade cycles
  • Project managers requiring independent contractor supervision

Our Electrical Consulting Services

Oleco’s electrical consulting offering covers the full project lifecycle, from initial concept through to completion and handover. Each engagement is tailored to the specific requirements of the project and the client’s internal capabilities.

Project Specification & Design Review

We develop detailed electrical specifications for new builds, refurbishments, and fit-outs. Where external designers or architects have produced electrical layouts, we provide independent design review to identify errors, inefficiencies, and compliance gaps before work begins. This early-stage intervention is where consulting delivers the greatest return — correcting a specification on paper costs a fraction of rectifying a completed installation.

Contractor Evaluation & Supervision

Selecting the right electrical contractor is critical to project success. We assist with tender evaluation, contractor vetting, and ongoing site supervision to ensure the chosen contractor delivers to the required standard. Our team conducts regular site inspections, progress reviews, and quality assessments throughout the project duration.

Compliance Advisory

UK electrical regulations are complex and subject to frequent updates. Our consultants provide clear, practical guidance on BS 7671 (IET Wiring Regulations), Building Regulations Part P, and sector-specific standards. We ensure your project is designed and delivered in full compliance, reducing the risk of enforcement action, insurance complications, or costly remedial work.

Cost Planning & Budget Management

Accurate cost planning at the outset prevents budget overruns during delivery. We provide detailed cost estimates, value engineering recommendations, and ongoing budget tracking. Our commercial awareness ensures clients achieve the required standard without unnecessary expenditure.

Electrical Project Management

For larger or more complex projects, Oleco provides full electrical project management. This includes programme development, coordination with other trades, resource planning, and stakeholder communication. We act as the single point of accountability for all electrical aspects of the project, ensuring seamless integration with the wider construction programme.

Frequently Asked Questions

Electrical Consulting FAQs

What does an electrical consultant do?
An electrical consultant provides independent technical advisory services for electrical projects. This includes project specification, design review, contractor evaluation and supervision, compliance advisory, cost planning, and ongoing project management. Unlike an electrical contractor who performs the physical installation, a consultant provides the strategic and technical guidance to ensure projects are designed correctly, delivered efficiently, and compliant with all relevant regulations.
When should I appoint an electrical consultant?
You should appoint an electrical consultant at the earliest stage of your project, ideally during the planning and design phase. Early engagement ensures the electrical specification is correct from the outset, prevents costly redesigns, and allows for accurate budgeting. Consultants are particularly valuable for new builds, major refurbishments, multi-site rollouts, and any project where the electrical scope is significant or complex.
Can Oleco provide consulting without performing the installation?
Yes. Our electrical consulting service is entirely independent of our contracting work. We regularly act as technical advisors for clients who have their own preferred contractors or who are required to tender work competitively. Our role in these cases is to ensure the specification is robust, the contractor selection is sound, and the finished installation meets the required standards.
What sectors do you provide electrical consulting for?
We provide electrical consulting services across all commercial and industrial sectors including hospitality, retail, healthcare, education, manufacturing, logistics, and corporate offices. We also work with specialist environments such as laboratories, data centres, and clean rooms. Our consultants are experienced with the specific regulatory and operational requirements of each sector.
How much does electrical consulting cost?
Consulting fees vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. We typically work on a fixed-fee basis for defined scopes of work, or a day-rate for ongoing advisory engagements.
