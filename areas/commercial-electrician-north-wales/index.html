Your Trusted Commercial Electrical Partner in North Wales

Oleco is a specialist commercial and industrial electrical contractor headquartered in North Wales. Since 2014, we have built a reputation for delivering technically demanding electrical projects across a wide range of sectors, from hospitality and tourism to manufacturing and public infrastructure. Our team operates from our base on the Menai Strait, placing us at the heart of the North Wales business community and within reach of every major commercial centre in the region.

North Wales is a region defined by its diversity. The coastline from Prestatyn to Holyhead supports a thriving tourism and hospitality economy, while inland areas host established industrial estates, manufacturing operations, and a growing number of technology-driven enterprises. Oleco understands the specific electrical demands of each sector and brings the technical depth required to deliver projects that meet the highest standards of compliance, performance, and design.

Commercial and Industrial Sectors We Serve

North Wales supports a broad mix of industries, and Oleco is proud to work across many of them. The tourism and hospitality sector is one of the region's most significant economic drivers, with hotels, restaurants, and leisure facilities along the coast demanding electrical installations that combine aesthetic excellence with operational reliability. We have completed high-profile hospitality projects across the region, delivering lighting and power systems that enhance guest experience while meeting rigorous safety standards.

The region's industrial estates, including those in Deeside, St Asaph, and the Parc Menai business park near Bangor, host manufacturing, logistics, and technology firms that require robust power infrastructure. Oleco regularly partners with these businesses to deliver distribution systems, control panels, and maintenance programmes that keep operations running efficiently.

Public sector organisations across North Wales, including local authorities, educational institutions, and healthcare facilities, also rely on Oleco for compliant and well-specified electrical work. We understand the procurement processes and compliance frameworks involved in public sector projects, and we deliver every engagement with full documentation and certification.
