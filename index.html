Our Services

Electrical Services for Business

From sophisticated lighting design to industrial power and control — Oleco delivers end-to-end electrical solutions for commercial and industrial clients.

Electrical Contracting

Full electrical installations for commercial and industrial premises. From new builds to refurbishments, we deliver projects on time and to the highest standards.

Lighting Design

Sophisticated lighting design that blends aesthetics with functionality. Hospitality, retail, commercial — we create spaces that look and feel exceptional.

Power & Control

Innovative power and control solutions for industrial environments. PLC systems, distribution boards, and bespoke control panels designed and installed.

Custom Installations
